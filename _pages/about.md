I am a research scientist at the Dalle Molle Institute for Artificial Intelligence (IDSIA USI-SUPSI), where I am affiliated with the [UBS-IDSIA AI Lab](https://ai-finance.idsia.ch/ubs-idsia-ai-lab/). Previously, I was with the Amazon Machine Learning and Forecasting Team in Berlin, and was a postdoctoral researcher at the School of Engineering, École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL). 

I received my PhD in Computer Science from EPFL in 2025. 

My research focuses on machine learning, signal processing, network science and causality. 

For more details, please find my [C.V. here](http://mertkayaalp.github.io/files/cv.pdf) (last updated December 2024).
